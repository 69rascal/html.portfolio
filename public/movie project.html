The Best movies According to Rascal

My top 3 movies of all-time

  • Highschool of The Dead

    This is my favourite anime. I love how they turn a zombie apocalyptic theme into an anime

  • Dune

    The new remake of Dune is one of The Best. I love how the stunning cinematography captures the vastness and harsh beauty of the desert planet Arrakis

  • Schindler's List

    Spielberg does not shy away from depicting the brutality and inhumanity of the Nazi regime, yet he also finds moments of humanity and hope in the midst of despair.
